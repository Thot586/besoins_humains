The nine fundamental human needs

Following the framework proposed by Manfred Max-Neef (1991) — nine universal needs, with no hierarchy between them, each feeding into the others. Every page explains one need: what it brings, the signs it is missing, the ways to cultivate it, and the pitfalls that lead away from it.
